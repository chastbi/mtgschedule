Welcome to the Meetings Group Calendar!


Instructions:

  • Presenter Schedule is for Rose and Julie to coordinate schedules. (Designed to mimic the current Excel spreadsheets and will require Scheduler permissions to edit.)
    - Data is automatically imported from the MRF database so that the calendar is always in-sync with actual MRF info.
    - Click on a calendar day to add a note to a presenter's schedule for that particular date. This is used for PTO, office days, and other non-MRF entries. Presenter will be made unavailable on that day and the following day on the Availability Calendar.
    - Click on note to edit or delete the note from the schedule.

  • Availability Calendar is for use by client teams to determine presenter availability.
    - Anywhere shows how many presenters can be available nearly anywhere to conduct meetings that day.
    - Cities listed show places where a presenter can realistically only conduct meetings in or around that city on that day due to other calendar obligations and/or travel requirements.
